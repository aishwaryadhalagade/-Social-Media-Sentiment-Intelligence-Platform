## Social Media Sentiment Intelligence

## Project Overview

The Social Media Sentiment Intelligence Platform is an AI-powered customer intelligence solution designed to analyze, monitor, and interpret customer sentiment across social media platforms, online reviews, forums, and digital conversations. By leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP), Transformer-based models, and Generative AI, the platform enables organizations to understand customer perceptions, identify emerging trends, monitor brand reputation, and drive data-driven business decisions.

The solution transforms unstructured customer feedback into actionable insights through automated sentiment classification, topic discovery, trend analysis, and brand health monitoring.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations receive thousands of customer interactions daily across social media platforms, review websites, forums, and customer feedback channels. Analyzing this unstructured data manually is time-consuming, inconsistent, and often fails to capture emerging customer concerns and opportunities.

Common business challenges include:

- Understanding customer sentiment at scale.
- Monitoring brand reputation across digital channels.
- Identifying emerging customer concerns and trends.
- Measuring campaign and product perception.
- Detecting shifts in customer behavior and preferences.
- Extracting actionable insights from large volumes of unstructured text.

Without automated sentiment intelligence, organizations risk delayed responses to customer issues, missed business opportunities, and reduced customer satisfaction.

---

## Solution

Developed an NLP-powered sentiment intelligence platform capable of analyzing customer reviews, social media conversations, survey responses, and digital feedback data across multiple channels.

Implemented advanced Transformer-based NLP models including BERT, RoBERTa, and DistilBERT to classify customer sentiment into positive, neutral, and negative categories. Leveraged Gemini AI for automated summarization, insight generation, trend identification, and executive reporting.

Built automated pipelines for text preprocessing, sentiment scoring, topic extraction, trend monitoring, and brand reputation analysis. Integrated interactive Power BI dashboards to provide real-time visibility into customer sentiment, emerging topics, competitor benchmarking, and customer experience metrics.

The platform enabled marketing, customer experience, and business teams to monitor customer perceptions and make proactive business decisions based on real-time customer intelligence.
